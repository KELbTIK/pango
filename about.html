The Team

Zion Harel

Chief Executive Officer
Zion has served as the CEO of Pango since 2008, leading corporate strategy and international business growth. He has over 25 years of international experience managing technology companies from early stage to product development and global sales. Zion spent six years in the USA as one of the founders and VP of Operations at Tefen USA (TASE: TEFEN), an international consulting firm for IT and management solutions. In addition, he has held senior positions at Fundtech Ltd. (NASDAQ: FNDT) and ESI, a Formula Vision company (NASDAQ: FORTY). Zion holds a B.Sc. in Industrial Engineering and Management from Ben-Gurion University.

Naji Sourani

Chief Executive Officer
Naji brings over 20 years of experience in software design, implementation and project management to Pango’s innovative technology development. As the leader of Pango’s R&D team, he has directed the development, implementation and deployment of the Pango platform around the world since 2006. Naji obtains unique expertise in security payment systems (PCIDSS1) and previously served as VP R&D at Emblaze-VCON. Naji holds a B.Sc. in Electronics Engineering from Ben-Gurion University.

Neil Edwards

Maoz Tenenbaum

Orna Eyal

Oren Brooks

Board of Directors

Prof. Yehuda Kahane

Chairman of the Board

Prof. Kahane is a senior member of faculty at the School of Management, Tel-Aviv University, and serves as the Head of the Akirov Institute for Business and the Environment. He has taught at major business schools including the Wharton School, University of Texas, University of Toronto, and University of Florida. He is a widely recognized authority in the areas of risk management, insurance, actuarial studies, and technological forecasting.

Prof. Kahane is the driving force behind numerous start-up enterprises in which he has been involved as founder,

investor and manager: He is a major shareholder and director at Capital Point Ltd., a high-tech incubator (listed on TASE), and the co-founder, director and a major shareholder at Ituran, an international leader in remote vehicle monitoring (NASDAQ: ITRN, TASE). He also served as a board member at New Dimension Software (now BMC Israel).

Prof. Kahane has been elected to be the recipient of the 2011 John S. Bickley Founder's Award, which is the highest and most prestigious award in the insurance world.

Dr. Yoram Romem

Board Member
Dr. Yoram Romem has more than 30 years of management experience in the global hi-tech industry. He has served as President, CEO and Chairman of international software companies, including Sapiens (NASDAQ: SPNS), ESI (acquired by Formula), Transtech (acquired by Formula), OmniVee (acquired by DVTel), and Profitect (a leading VC-backed global Enterprise Software company). He served as the CIO of the Israeli Air Force in the rank of colonel. Yoram was one

of the founders of Pango Mobile Parking Ltd, and he currently serves as a Chairman of several advanced hi-tech companies.

Yoram holds a B.Sc. Summa Cum Laude in Computer Sciences from the Technion, Israel Institute of Technology, and a Ph.D. in Philosophy of Science from the Bar-Ilan University.

Michael (Miki) Ron

Board Member
Since 1999, Miki has been active in initiating and promoting Israeli technology startups in the medical device, biotechnology, communications and automotive fields. He has established and manages a startup investment club, and serves as an Executive Board Director in a technology incubator. Miki held senior technological positions at the Israel Atomic Energy Commission, where he was employed for a period of 22 years. In his capacity as VP Technology in a large Israeli company, he was responsible for industrial projects involving renewable energy. Miki started and managed the company

that imports and distributes the Japanese Mazda cars, which has become the largest and most profitable car business in Israel.

Miki holds Bachelor of Science and Master of Science degrees in Electrical Power Engineering, and a Master of Science degree in Industrial Engineering and Management.

Dr. Shmuel Cabilly

Board Member

(Ph.D., Immunology), and was a Post-Doctoral fellow at the City of Hope Research Institute heading a joint City of Hope/Genentech project where he developed a new technology for recombinant antibody production.

Yoav Kahane

Board Member
Mr. Kahane is leading a start-up company in the field of medical device while additionally offering business development services as a consultant. Mark Fischer
